Beyerdynamic DT-990 Pro


How did I get it?

I was looking through retail prices for this headphone.
I realised it was better to look though Carousell - a place where people sell and buy stuff online. It is much cheaper to buy used.

And that's how I got it.



Why did I get it?

I wanted an open pair of headphones that fit best with anything I was listening to. At the time, I had a large catalogue of Electronic music.
Bam! This pair came at the right time and it was perfect for the genre. Bass is its strong point together with a wide sound.

And that's why I got it.
