Fostex T50rp


How did I get it?

A friend of mine offered me a good price.
I decided to go at the deal and bought it.
It really is a good starting headphone at a cheap price.

And that's how I got it.



Why did I get it?

I was looking for a planar magnetic headphone.
A planar magnetic headphone has a different driver design, therefore, different sound.
It has a very wide soundstage and the bass is deep and extends very far.
Though I should say, this headphones fits a lot of genres not just one.

And that's why I got it.
